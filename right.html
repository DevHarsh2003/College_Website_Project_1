WELCOME TO ANITS

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences Visakhapatnam also known as ANITS VISAKHAPATNAM, is a Private (Autonomous) College, affiliated to AU VISAKHAPATNAM situated in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. ANITS VISAKHAPATNAM was established in the year 2001. Its campus is spread over 12 Acres of land. ANITS VISAKHAPATNAM is accredited by NAAC A, NBA and approved by AICTE. ANITS VISAKHAPATNAM offers admission in various courses like BE, B.Tech, ME, M.Tech. Various Facilities provided by ANITS VISAKHAPATNAM are Wi-Fi, Medical Support Systems, Canteen, Conference Hall, Boys Hostel, Girls Hostel, Transport Facility, Library & Information Center, IT Centers and etc.
